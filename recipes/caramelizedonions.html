Caramelized Onions

Description

Caramelized onions for use as a topping on whatever you'd like.

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 yellow, white, or red onions
  • 3 to 4 tablespoons olive oil or unsalted butter (or ideally, a combination of the two)
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of red wine vinegar, balsamic vinegar, or wine

Steps

  1. Cut the stem and root ends off onions, then halve the onion and cut into thin slices.
  2. Add three to four tablespoons of fat to a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add onions and salt to skillet.
  4. Stir and continue stirring onions slowly until golden brown.
